Types of Bikes

Mountain Bikes

Mountain bikes are designed for off-road cycling. They typically feature a sturdy build and specialized features for tackling rough terrains.

Last updated: October 20, 2023
Road Bikes

Road bikes are designed for use on paved roads. They are built for speed and efficiency, with lightweight frames and narrow tires.

Last updated: October 20, 2023
Gravel Bikes

Gravel bikes are versatile, combining elements of road and mountain bikes. They are designed for riding on various surfaces, including gravel roads, dirt paths, and pavement.

Last updated: October 20, 2023
